“Han Cung Xuan Hieu Do” Painting

Han Cung Xuan Xiaotu" (early morning in the Han palace), whic has 37.2 cm in width and 2038.5 cm length, by artist Cuu Anh, is the first long scroll style painting of Chinese painting. He was a typical artist of the Ming Dynasty, along with Than Chau, Duong Dan, and Van Thuy Minh, he was revered as the "Four masterpieces" of Ming Dynasty painting. the best of Cuu Anh in his art lifetime.
